# Food Plan: Simple & Sustainable

*Part of the Definite Plan for the Health Desire Statement. Simple so it’s easy to stick to.*

---

## Intermittent fasting (once per week)

- **When:** Sunday evening → Monday (24–30 hours).
- **Format:** Last meal Sunday evening; next meal Monday evening (or late afternoon if you prefer 24 hours).
- **During fast:** Water, black coffee, plain tea. No calories.
- Adjust the window if another day works better (e.g. Saturday evening → Sunday).

---

## Simple meal framework (no overthinking)

**Base formula:** Protein + vegetables + optional starch.

- **Protein:** Chicken (grilled, baked, rotisserie), plus eggs, fish, or other lean protein when you want variety.
- **Vegetables:** Salads and cooked vegetables (raw, steamed, roasted, stir-fried).
- **Starch (optional):** Rice, potato, sweet potato, or bread when you want more fuel.

---

## Meal ideas (rotate and repeat)

### Lunch
- **Big chicken salad:** Greens, chicken (leftover or pre-cooked), veggies, simple dressing (oil/vinegar or lemon).
- **Chicken + side salad + optional rice or potato.**
- **Repeat:** Same combo most days; change dressings or veg to avoid boredom.

### Dinner
- **Chicken + vegetables:** Baked or grilled chicken, large portion of vegetables (e.g. broccoli, zucchini, greens, carrots).
- **Salad with chicken:** Same as lunch, different greens or toppings.
- **Fish + vegetables** (e.g. 1–2 nights a week for variety).

### Breakfast (non-fast days)
- **Simple:** Eggs (scrambled, boiled, or omelet) + vegetables or side salad.
- **Or:** Greek yogurt + fruit + optional nuts.
- **Or:** Skip and do a later lunch if that fits better.

---

## “Don’t think about it” tactics

1. **Batch cook:** Grill or bake several chicken breasts (and maybe a tray of vegetables) 1–2x per week; use for lunches and dinners.
2. **Same shopping list:** Chicken, eggs, salad greens, 3–4 vegetables you like, olive oil, vinegar/lemon, salt/pepper. Buy the same core items each week.
3. **Default plate:** Half plate vegetables, quarter chicken (or other protein), quarter starch (if any). No measuring needed.
4. **One “no decision” rule:** e.g. “Lunch is always chicken salad unless I’m traveling or have a special plan.”
